What you sell

Apa yang anda jual

You sell custom websites for Malaysian businesses — designed from zero for each client, in English and Bahasa Malaysia, with an admin panel so the owner updates things themselves. You are not selling "coding". You are selling: more customers, more trust, less repetitive WhatsApp questions.

Your one-liner — memorise it / Hafal ayat ni

EN: "I build custom websites for Malaysian businesses — with an admin panel so you can update everything yourself. Launch pricing for my first 10 clients." BM: "Saya bina website custom untuk bisnes Malaysia — siap panel admin, boss boleh update sendiri. Harga pelancaran untuk 10 klien pertama."

Important: never say "AI website" or "siap dalam sejam". Cheap AI-site sellers say that. You show working demos with admin panels — that's your proof of quality. Let the work speak.

Finding customers

Macam mana nak cari pelanggan

The golden rule: contact 10 businesses every day. Selling is a numbers game — out of 100 contacts, expect ~5–10 conversations and 1–3 clients. That is normal and enough: 2 clients/month = RM 2,000–4,000+.

Channel 1 — People you know (start here, closes best)

  • Post on WhatsApp Status + personal IG/FB: your one-liner + demo link. Do this today.
  • Message 10 friends/family who run businesses — personal message, mention their shop by name.

Channel 2 — Google Maps hunting (your daily 10)

  1. Open Google Maps → search "kedai kopi / barber / klinik / gym / bengkel" in your area
  2. Look for businesses with good reviews but NO website link — they are proven businesses missing the one thing you sell
  3. Find their number (Maps profile, IG bio, or call) → send the opener script (Section D)
  4. Record every contact in a notebook/sheet: name, shop, date, status

Channel 3 — Walk-ins (2–3 per week, highest close rate)

Visit at quiet hours (cafés 3–5pm). Buy something small. Then: "Boss, kedai ni cantik. Saya buat website untuk bisnes macam ni — jap saya tunjuk satu contoh…" — open the matching demo, hand them your phone, show them "Try the admin". The phone in their hands does the selling.

Channel 4 — Facebook groups & TikTok (slow burn, do weekly)

  • Join "Peniaga [your town]" / "Usahawan Malaysia" groups. Help people; share demos only when relevant.
  • TikTok formats that work: screen-record a demo with BM voiceover; "before/after" website glow-ups; "boss tukar harga menu dalam 10 saat" admin clips.

Talking to customers — the scripts

1 · Cold opener (WhatsApp, BM)

Salam boss 🙏 Saya Adiff, saya buat website untuk bisnes Malaysia. Saya nampak [NAMA KEDAI] kat Google — review memang bagus, tapi takde website lagi. Saya ada demo website kedai [kopi/makan/klinik] yang boleh boss cuba sendiri — termasuk panel admin dia. Nak saya hantar link? Tengok je, tak semestinya beli 😊

2 · They reply "ok hantar" — send this

Ni dia boss 👉 zed-studio-theta.vercel.app Scroll ke "Demos", pilih yang sama macam bisnes boss. Cuba tekan "Try the admin" — boss boleh tukar harga/menu dan tengok dia berubah terus kat website. Kalau nak yang macam ni untuk [NAMA KEDAI], saya boleh buat. 😊

3 · No reply after 2 days — one follow-up only

Boss, sempat tengok demo tu tak? Kalau sibuk takpe — saya tinggalkan sini je: harga pelancaran saya start RM 990, untuk 10 klien pertama sahaja. Bila-bila boss ready, WhatsApp saya 🙏

4 · They're interested — the quote

Untuk [NAMA KEDAI], saya cadangkan pakej [Landing Page RM 990 / Business Website RM 2,190 / Custom RM 3,990]. Harga tetap — takde caj tersembunyi. Dalam tu termasuk [design custom, versi BI+BM, panel admin, …]. Siap dalam [1 minggu / 2–3 minggu]. Saya hantar PDF penuh ye — ada semua detail.

5 · The close — never ask "nak buat tak?"

Kalau boss ok, saya boleh mula minggu ni. Proses dia senang: (1) boss sahkan pakej, (2) deposit 50%, (3) saya design homepage dulu untuk boss approve, (4) siap penuh, bayar baki, website live. Semua dalam surat perjanjian satu muka surat — senang baca. Saya hantar sekarang?

Rules of talking: always use their shop name · one question per message · reply within 2 hours · never argue, agree then explain · never drop your price — add value instead ("Saya tak boleh turunkan harga, tapi saya boleh tambah satu section galeri percuma").
E

Handling objections

Bila dorang bagi alasan — jawapan sedia
"Saya dah ada TikTok / Instagram" — THE big one

Betul boss, TikTok memang penting — itu untuk orang JUMPA boss. Tapi TikTok macam sewa gerai: algorithm tentukan siapa nampak, dan semua profile nampak sama. Website macam kedai sendiri — bila orang Google "[jenis bisnes] near me", website yang keluar, bukan TikTok. Dan customer check website dulu sebelum spend duit besar. Dua-dua kena ada: TikTok bawa crowd, website tukar crowd jadi customer.

"Mahal la"

Saya faham boss. Tapi kira macam ni: website builder bulanan tu RM 400 sebulan — setahun RM 4,800, dan bayar selamanya. Saya punya sekali bayar je, website tu milik boss. Agency pulak caj RM 5,000 ke atas untuk kerja custom macam ni. RM 990 ni harga pelancaran — lepas 10 klien pertama, naik.

"Nanti dulu / saya fikir dulu"

Takpe boss, tak rush 🙏 Satu je saya mintak — cuba demo tu 2 minit. Lepas tu kalau rasa nak, WhatsApp je. Cuma harga pelancaran ni untuk 10 bisnes pertama — kalau berminat jangan tunggu lama sangat 😊

"Buat free dulu boleh? / tunjuk design dulu"

Design memang boss tengok dulu — homepage siap dulu, boss approve baru saya teruskan. Tapi kerja mula lepas deposit ya, sebab setiap design saya buat dari kosong khas untuk kedai boss. Demo yang boss dah cuba tu — itulah "free sample" saya 😊

"Adik saya boleh buat guna Wix"

Boleh je! Kalau adik boss sempat maintain, ok. Bezanya kerja saya: custom penuh, takde template, loading laju, ada panel admin senang, siap SEO. Kalau satu hari nak upgrade, saya ada. Pintu sentiasa terbuka 🙏

Promoting yourself

Cara promosi — percuma
  • WhatsApp Status (daily-ish): demo clips, "slot launch pricing tinggal X", work-in-progress shots once you have clients.
  • TikTok/IG Reels (2–3/week, 20–40s, BM voiceover): screen-record demos, admin panel magic ("tukar harga dalam 10 saat"), before/after. Always end: "Link kat bio — cuba admin dia sekali".
  • Facebook groups: answer website questions helpfully. People buy from the helpful person.
  • Every finished client site is a walking advertisement — ask for the testimonial + tag.
What NOT to do: no paid ads yet (waste of money before 10 clients) · no spam blasting identical messages (numbers get reported) · no discounting below launch price — scarcity only works if it's real.

H

Working with clients, step by step

Aliran kerja dengan klien
  1. Day 0 — Close: agreement signed, deposit received. Send: "Terima kasih boss! Saya mula esok. Saya perlukan beberapa benda…" + the content checklist below.
  2. Day 1–2 — Collect content: logo (or shop photo of signboard), 10–20 photos, menu/services + prices, address & hours, phone, social links, "about" story (3–4 sentences — interview them on WhatsApp if they can't write it).
  3. Day 2–4 — Homepage first: build the homepage, send a screenshot or preview link: "Ni first look boss — apa rasa?" Adjust until approved (this is revision round 1).
  4. Day 4–10 — Full build: all pages, both languages, admin panel. Send progress updates every 2–3 days even if small — silence makes clients nervous.
  5. Review — rounds 2 & 3: client checks everything on their own phone. Fix, polish.
  6. Launch: collect final 50% → put the site live on their domain → walkthrough video of their admin (screen-record 5 min) → handover credentials.
  7. Day +14: check in: "Semua ok boss? Ada apa-apa nak baiki?" Then ask for the testimonial (Section L) and offer the Care Plan (Section K).
Content checklist to send clients (copy-paste):
"Boss, untuk mula saya perlukan: (1) logo atau gambar signboard, (2) 10–20 gambar kedai/produk, (3) senarai menu/servis + harga, (4) alamat + waktu buka, (5) nombor telefon + link IG/FB, (6) cerita ringkas pasal bisnes boss (3–4 ayat). Hantar sini je bila ready 🙏"

  1. Always 50% before any work, 50% before the site goes live. No exceptions — not even friends. The demos are the free sample.

Pelan penjagaan — duit bulanan anda

A Care Plan is a monthly subscription where you look after the client's website. This is how the business becomes stable — 10 clients on Essential = RM 790/month recurring, before any new project.
