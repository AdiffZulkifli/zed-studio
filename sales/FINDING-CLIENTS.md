# Finding Clients — 30-Day Action Plan

The exact daily actions to get Zed Studio's first clients, built for the Malaysian scene.
Scripts live in `docs/SALES-PLAYBOOK.md`. Send prospects `Zed-Studio-Pitch.pdf`. Close with `Zed-Studio-Agreement.pdf`.

**The only rule that matters: outreach every single day.** Courses like BossNizam's sell one secret — consistency with a script. You already have better tools than their students (live demos, a wizard, a pitch PDF); the missing ingredient is only daily reps.

## Week 1 — Warm circle + setup (target: 2 serious conversations)
- [ ] Install **WhatsApp Business** on +60 11-6329 3004: business profile, logo, packages in catalog, away message ("Terima kasih! Kami balas dalam 24 jam"), quick replies for the demo link and pricing
- [ ] Post on WhatsApp Status + personal IG/FB: "Saya buat website untuk bisnes Malaysia. 10 client pertama dapat harga pelancaran. Cuba demo ni — boleh test admin dia sekali: zed-studio-theta.vercel.app"
- [ ] DM 10 people you know who run/whose family runs a business. Personal message each, never copy-paste blast
- [ ] Grab @zedstudio (or nearest) on Instagram + TikTok — even before posting, own the name

## Week 2 — Direct prospecting begins (target: 30 cold contacts)
- [ ] **Google Maps method, 10/day:** search your area for cafés, barbers, clinics, gyms, workshops → filter for good reviews + NO website link → send the BM cold opener from the playbook, naming their shop specifically
- [ ] **Walk-ins, 2–3 this week:** visit nearby shops at quiet hours. Open the matching demo on your phone, hand it over, let them tap "Try the admin". The phone-in-their-hands moment closes better than any message
- [ ] Track every contact in a simple note or sheet: name, shop, date, status (sent / replied / demo'd / quoted / closed)

## Week 3 — Content flywheel starts (runs alongside daily outreach)
- [ ] 2–3 TikTok/IG Reels per week, 20–40s each, BM voiceover. Formats that work:
  - "Kedai [type] ini takde website… so ni contoh apa saya boleh buat" → screen-record scrolling a demo
  - Before/after: ugly generic site vs. your demo
  - "Boss tukar harga menu sendiri dalam 10 saat" → screen-record the admin panel edit appearing live on the site
- [ ] Every video ends: "Link demo kat bio — cuba sendiri, admin pun boleh test"
- [ ] Join 3–5 local business Facebook groups (Peniaga/Usahawan groups). Contribute answers; share demos only when someone asks about websites

## Week 4 — Follow-up week (most sales close here)
- [ ] Re-contact every Week-2 prospect who didn't reply (2-day-gap follow-up script)
- [ ] Every prospect who tried a demo gets: "Boss sempat cuba demo tu? Apa-apa soalan saya ada 😊"
- [ ] Anyone hesitating gets the scarcity nudge: slots under launch pricing are filling
- [ ] Review the tracker: which channel produced conversations? Double down on that one in month 2

## Numbers to expect (be patient, this is normal)
Cold WhatsApp: ~10–20% reply, ~5% become conversations. 100 contacts ≈ 5–10 real conversations ≈ 1–3 clients. At RM 990–2,190 each, month one can realistically pay for the domain, SSM, and your time. Warm circle converts 5–10× better — which is why it's week 1.

## When a lead arrives (from wizard, DM, or walk-in)
1. Reply within 2 hours if humanly possible (24h max — it's our public promise)
2. Qualify: industry, stage, what they sell → send the matching demo + pitch PDF
3. Verbal yes → agreement PDF → signed photo → 50% deposit → only then build
4. Check `zs_leads` in Supabase every morning — wizard leads may not press the WhatsApp button
