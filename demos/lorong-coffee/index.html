Our Story

Where Every Lane Leads to Good Coffee

"Lorong" — the Malay word for lane or alley — captures the spirit of our café perfectly. We're that hidden gem down a familiar street, a cosy corner where good coffee and warm company come together.

We started Lorong Coffee with one belief: specialty coffee should belong to everyone. No overpriced lattes, no intimidating menus — just honest, quality brews crafted with passion in the heart of Sarawak.

From our signature iced drinks to rich hot brews, every cup is a testament to our love for the craft and our community.
