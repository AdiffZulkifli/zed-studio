Our Story

We Are Lorong Coffee

A small café with a big heart — born from a love of coffee and community, right here in Sarawak.

How It All Began

From a Passion for Coffee to Sarawak's Favourite Café

It started with a simple question: why does specialty coffee have to be so expensive? The founders of Lorong Coffee — a small team of Sarawakians who grew up drinking kopitiam coffee — believed the answer was simple: it doesn't.

In 2022, they opened their first outlet in Bintulu. The bright yellow walls, the green logo, the warm welcome — it all reflected what they stood for: coffee that's affordable, quality that's uncompromising, and a space that truly belongs to the community.

"Lorong" — meaning lane or alley in Malay — was the perfect name. Every great lorong has its hidden gem, a place you stumble upon and can't stop returning to. That's what we aim to be.

Today, with multiple outlets across Bintulu, Lorong Coffee continues to grow. But our heart remains the same: every cup crafted with care, every customer welcomed like a regular.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

🌱
Quality Without Compromise

We source ethically grown beans and roast with precision. Every cup you drink reflects our obsession with flavour and craft.

🤝
Community First

We're not just a coffee shop — we're a gathering place. From regulars who know us by name to first-timers exploring, everyone is welcome here.

💚
Affordability Matters

Premium coffee shouldn't be a privilege. We price our drinks so that everyone in Sarawak can enjoy a quality brew every single day.

🌏
Sustainability

We minimise waste, support eco-friendly practices, and strive to run a business that gives more back than it takes.

✨
Warmth & Belonging

Walk in once and you'll feel it — the cheerful atmosphere, the familiar smells, the genuine smiles. That's Lorong Coffee.

🚀
Always Innovating

Seasonal specials, local collaborations, and new flavour experiments — we never stop evolving our menu and customer experience.
