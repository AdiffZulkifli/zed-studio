How It Works

1
Register Free

Sign up with your name, phone number, and email. Takes 30 seconds.

2
Tell Staff Your Number

When ordering, give your phone number to the branch staff. They'll log your purchase.

3

After 10 recorded cups, your free drink reward is ready. Check your progress anytime online.

4
Redeem — Then Repeat

Tell the staff you want to redeem. Enjoy your free drink. Counter resets and the fun starts again.



Good to Know

Reward Rules

📱
Phone Number Required

Always give your registered phone number when ordering so the branch can log your purchase to your account.

🔄
Redeem Before Buying More

Once your reward is ready, you must redeem it before more purchases are added to your account.

🏪
Valid at All Branches

Your stamp card works at every Lorong Coffee branch. Mix and match — a cup from any branch counts.

☕
Any Drink Counts

Every drink purchase counts toward your reward, regardless of size or type. Redeem for any available menu item.
