About MedicMesir

17 years of purpose-driven medical education consulting — built on one promise: every student who walks through our doors will become a doctor.

Our Mission

Serving Malaysia Through
the Gift of Doctors

MedicMesir (MM) was established in 2007 with a single, powerful goal: to dramatically increase the number of doctors serving Malaysia. For too long, talented students lacked the guidance to pursue medicine abroad.

We changed that. Through meticulous counselling, strong university partnerships, and unwavering post-graduation support, MM has produced thousands of doctors who now serve on the frontlines of Malaysian healthcare. Since founding, not a single MM graduate has gone without placement in Malaysia — a record we intend to uphold forever.

Our Story

17 Years of Milestones

From a single office to a nationwide network — here's how we grew.

2007

MedicMesir Founded

Established in Seri Kembangan with a small team of dedicated counsellors and big dreams for Malaysian healthcare.

2010

First 500 Graduates

Our first major cohort of graduates returned to Malaysia — all successfully placed in government hospitals.

2015

Expanded to 6 Countries

We broadened our university network to include Russia, Indonesia, Jordan, Bulgaria, and more.

2020

10,000 Graduates Milestone

A landmark moment — 10,000 doctors produced across all programmes, 100% placement rate intact.

2025

Expanding Nationwide

New counselling centres opened in Johor Bahru and Kota Kinabalu to serve students across Malaysia.
