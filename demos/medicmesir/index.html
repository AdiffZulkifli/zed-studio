Who We Are

Dedicated to Growing
Malaysia's Medical Community

MedicMesir (MM) was founded in 2007 with a singular mission — to increase the number of doctors serving Malaysia. Over 17 years, we have produced thousands of doctors who now stand on the frontlines of healthcare across the nation.

Not a single MM graduate has gone without placement back in Malaysia. We are proud of that legacy and remain committed to making that record unbroken.

How It Works

Your Journey With Us

From your first enquiry to your first day of practice — we guide you through every milestone.
