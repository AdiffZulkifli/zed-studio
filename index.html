06

Common questions

How long does a website take?

A landing page takes about a week. A full business website takes 2–3 weeks. Custom systems depend on scope — we'll give you a clear timeline with your quote.

Can I update the website myself?

Yes. Business and Custom packages include a private admin panel where you can edit menus, prices, photos and news yourself — no technical skills needed.

Do you provide the domain and hosting?

Yes — setup is RM 150 one-time, then RM 150/year for domain management: that yearly fee includes your .com or .my domain cost, renewal and DNS care. The domain is registered under your name — you always own it, we just manage it so you never think about it.

What happens after launch?

Every project includes a support window for fixes. If you want ongoing updates, backups and monitoring, our Care Plans start at RM 79/month.

What exactly is a Care Plan — Essential vs Pro?

A Care Plan is optional monthly upkeep after launch. Essential (RM 79/month) covers a monthly health check, a backup, and up to 2 content updates done for you. Pro (RM 149/month) adds up to 8 updates monthly, one new section or promo banner every quarter, and a monthly WhatsApp report of your visits and enquiries. Month-to-month, cancel anytime with 14 days' notice — and without one, your site still runs fine; you update it yourself via your admin panel.

Why not just use a template or page builder?

Templates look like everyone else, load slowly, and lock you into monthly platform fees. A custom-built site is faster, unique to your brand, and fully yours.
