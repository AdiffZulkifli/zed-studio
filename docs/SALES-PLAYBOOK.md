# Zed Studio — Sales Playbook

How to find customers, what to say, and how to close — Malaysian SME context.
Companion files: `sales/Zed-Studio-Pitch.pdf` (send to prospects), `sales/Zed-Studio-Agreement.pdf` (sign before starting).

## The market position (memorise this)
Malaysian pricing reality (2026): DIY builders RM 379–479/month forever · template freelancers RM 1,500–6,000 · studios RM 3,000–10,000 · agencies RM 8,000–25,000+. **Zed Studio delivers studio-grade custom work at RM 990–3,990, fixed.** We are not the cheapest option (a template freelancer can undercut us) — we are the best value for *custom* work. Never compete on price with template sellers; compete on "made only for you + you control it yourself."

## Where to find customers (in order of conversion rate)

1. **Warm circle first.** WhatsApp Status + personal FB/IG post: "I build websites for Malaysian businesses — first 10 get launch pricing. Here's a live demo you can actually play with: [link]". Friends-of-friends close fastest.
2. **Walk-in prospecting.** Cafés, barbers, clinics, gyms near you with no website (check Google first). Show the matching demo ON YOUR PHONE, let *them* tap around the admin. The demo does the selling.
3. **Google Maps prospecting.** Search an area for businesses with good reviews but no website link → WhatsApp them (script below). 10 messages/day, every day.
4. **Facebook groups.** Local business/community groups ("Peniaga [town]", "Usahawan..."). Don't spam — answer questions, show demos when relevant.
5. **TikTok content** (later, when time allows): 30s screen-recordings — "kedai kopi ini takde website, so saya buat satu untuk demo" format. Builds inbound.

## Scripts

### Cold WhatsApp opener (BM — use for most SMEs)
> Salam boss 🙏 Saya Adiff, saya buat website untuk bisnes Malaysia. Saya nampak [nama kedai] kat Google — review memang bagus, tapi takde website lagi. Saya ada demo website kedai [kopi/makan/klinik] yang boleh boss cuba sendiri — termasuk panel admin dia. Nak saya hantar link? Tengok je, tak semestinya beli 😊

Rules: name their business specifically (never blast identical messages), one question at the end, no price in the first message.

### Follow-up (if seen, no reply — wait 2 days)
> Boss, ni link demo tu: [demo URL]. Cuba tekan "Try the admin" — boss boleh tukar harga menu dan tengok dia berubah terus kat website. Kalau berminat nak yang macam ni untuk [nama kedai], harga pelancaran saya start RM 990 (10 client pertama sahaja).

### The close
Never "so, nak buat tak?" — instead:
> Kalau boss ok, saya boleh mulakan minggu ni. Deposit 50%, siap dalam [1 minggu / 2-3 minggu], harga tetap — semua dalam surat perjanjian satu muka surat, senang baca. Saya hantar sekarang?

## Objection handling

### "Saya dah ada TikTok/Instagram" (THE big one)
Never argue that social media is bad — agree, then reframe:
> Betul boss, TikTok memang penting — itu untuk orang *jumpa* boss. Tapi TikTok tu macam sewa gerai kat pasar malam: algorithm yang tentukan siapa nampak, dan profile semua orang nampak sama. Website pula macam kedai sendiri: bila orang Google "[jenis bisnes] near me", yang keluar website — bukan TikTok. Dan sebelum customer spend duit besar, dorang check: ada website ke? Kalau ada, terus nampak professional, harga pun boleh letak lebih yakin. Dua-dua kena ada: TikTok bawa crowd, website tukar crowd jadi customer.
Then show, don't tell: open the matching demo, hand them the phone.

### "Mahal la" 
> Saya faham. Tapi cuba kira: website builder macam Wix/tempahan bulanan tu RM 400 sebulan — setahun dah RM 4,800, dan tiap tahun bayar lagi. Saya punya sekali bayar je, website tu milik boss selamanya. Dan agency biasa caj RM 5,000 ke atas untuk kerja custom macam ni. RM 990 ni harga pelancaran — lepas 10 client pertama, naik.

### "Nanti dulu / tak sempat nak fikir"
> Takpe boss, tak rush. Satu je saya mintak — cuba demo tu 2 minit, tekan "Try the admin". Lepas tu kalau rasa nak, WhatsApp saya je. Harga pelancaran ni untuk 10 bisnes pertama, so kalau berminat jangan tunggu lama sangat 😊
(Scarcity is real — we genuinely raise prices after 10.)

### "Boleh buat free dulu tak / kasi tengok design dulu"
> Design memang boss tengok dulu sebelum saya build penuh — homepage siap dulu, boss approve baru saya teruskan. Tapi kerja bermula lepas deposit ya, sebab setiap design saya buat dari kosong khas untuk [nama kedai].
Never work before deposit. The demos ARE the free sample.

### "Adik saya boleh buat guna Wix"
> Boleh! Kalau adik boss sempat maintain, itu pun ok. Bezanya: kerja saya custom — takde template, loading laju, ada admin panel senang, dan siap SEO. Kalau satu hari nak upgrade, saya ada. Pintu sentiasa terbuka 🙏

## Process discipline
1. Every enquiry answered within 24h (aim: 2h).
2. Qualified prospect → send pitch PDF + relevant demo link.
3. Verbal yes → send agreement PDF, get it signed (photo of signed page on WhatsApp is fine), collect 50% deposit.
4. Only then start building. No exceptions, even for friends.
5. At launch → ask for the testimonial + portfolio permission (launch pricing is the trade).
